Fastnet Project

Design that responds to the landscape and is rooted in local tradition.

A place-based project set against the Fastnet Rock and the coastline off the south of Cork — where the lighthouse, the stone walls and the shoreline set the terms for the design.

The work begins from two principles: respond to the landscape and stay rooted in local tradition — letting the materials, weather and edges of the place shape what gets built.
