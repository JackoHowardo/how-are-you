Studio — design, research & play

I make things that ask how are you?

I'm Jack Howard, a Cork-based designer working between furniture, interiors and making. I'm drawn to objects and places that make people pause — a seat for the city steps, a fog-catcher for an island, a film about wanting somewhere dry to sit. This side is the studio: the self-directed, curious, slightly playful half of the practice, and where it's all trying to go.

Services — for clients & collaborators

Need something made?

I design and build furniture, interiors, set pieces and bespoke one-offs — from the first sketch to the finished, installed object. I've built workshop furniture and stage sets in Granada, public furniture for Copenhagen, and interior concepts for cafés, retail and community spaces. Tell me what you're trying to make and where it lives, and I'll help get it into the real world.
